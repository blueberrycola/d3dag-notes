Why Visualize Data?
computer based vis systems provide visual representations of datasets to help people carry out tasks more effectively.
Visualization is suitable when there is a need to augment human capabilities rather than replace people with computation decision making methods
NOTE: vis isnt needed when fully automatic solutions exist and is trusted

  • Presentation of known results
  • Used to build ML model
    Why use an external representation?
    External representation: replace cognition with perception

Which is fancy speak for why try to find the inner workings of a problem when you can try to let a computer show you

An example: global religious landscape of India

Dataset types with d3:

  • Tables {items:rows, attributes:cols}
  • Multidimensional Table {Key 1, Key2, Attributes = Value in Cell}
  • Networks: link nodes, and items
  • Trees: Like networks but hierarchal
  • Spatial: Fields and Geometry

Attribute Types:
Categorical: column of a table
Ordinal: Natural Ordering {Small, Medium, Large}
Quantitative: Numbers/Quantities
Special cases:
Time:
Point in time: quantitative
Region in time: quantitative
Space:
Point in space
Region of space

Example: religionByCountry.csv
Categorical: country, religion
Quantitative: population
